Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

This app accesses the following data from your Spotify account using the official Spotify API:

  • Your email and account information (user-read-email, user-read-private)
  • Your playlists (playlist-read-private, playlist-read-collaborative)
  • Your currently playing track and playback state (via App Remote SDK)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your playlists and currently playing track within the app
  • To allow you to control playback on your Spotify account
  • No data is stored permanently by the app
  • No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses Spotify services to fetch data and control playback. Your data is handled according to Spotify’s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

  • All communication with Spotify is encrypted via HTTPS
  • OAuth with PKCE is used to securely authenticate your account
  • No sensitive credentials (like your Spotify password) are stored on the device

5. User Control

  • You can revoke app access at any time in your Spotify account settings
  • You can uninstall the app at any time

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact me at: your.email@example.com
