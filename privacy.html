Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Data We Access

This app accesses the following data from your Spotify account using the official Spotify Web API:

  • Your playlists (playlist-read-private, playlist-read-collaborative)

2. How Your Data Is Used

  • To display your playlists inside the app
  • No data is stored permanently
  • No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses Spotify services to retrieve playlist information. Your data is handled according to Spotify’s Privacy Policy: https://www.spotify.com/us/legal/privacy-policy/

4. Data Security

  • All communication with Spotify is encrypted via HTTPS
  • OAuth with PKCE is used for secure authentication
  • No Spotify passwords or sensitive credentials are stored

5. User Control

  • You can revoke app access at any time in your Spotify account settings
  • You can uninstall the app at any time
